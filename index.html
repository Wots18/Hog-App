What We Offer

Built for Africa.
Ready for the World.

Every feature designed to solve real problems Africans face with money movement — from mobile money to global bank accounts.

Mobile Money Integration

Send and receive directly from MTN MoMo, Telecel Cash, and AirtelTigo Money. No bank account required to get started.

Pan-African Transfers

Send money to 54 African countries in seconds. Nigeria, Kenya, Senegal, South Africa — all covered with real-time exchange rates.

Virtual USD Account

Get a real US bank account number. Receive USD payments, salaries from abroad, and international transfers like a global citizen.

Multi-Currency Wallet

Hold GHS, USD, EUR, GBP, NGN, and more in one place. Swap between currencies at live market rates with minimal fees.

Bank-Grade Security

AES-256 encryption, 2-factor authentication, real-time fraud monitoring, and instant transaction alerts keep your money safe.

Instant Settlements

No more waiting 3-5 business days. HOG transactions settle in real time — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year.

Our Story

Born in Ghana.
Built for Africa.

HOG was founded with one purpose: to make money movement as borderless as ideas. We watched Ghanaians pay 10–15% in fees to send money to a neighbouring country. We decided that had to change.

We are regulated, secure, and committed to being the financial backbone of African commerce — from market traders in Accra to tech workers receiving salaries in dollars.

Trust First

Your money is safe. Always. Licensed and regulated by the Bank of Ghana.

Radical Transparency

No hidden fees. No fine print. What you see is exactly what you pay.

Built Locally

Ghanaian team, Ghanaian focus, African vision. We understand this market.

Globally Ambitious

Starting in Ghana, expanding across Africa, aiming at the world.
